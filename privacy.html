Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 27, 2026 · Last Updated: March 27, 2026

1. Introduction

WatchLocal.ai ("we," "us," or "our") operates watchlocal.ai and related services. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data. We are committed to transparency and minimal data collection.

2. Information We Collect

Business Information: When you sign up for our service, we collect your business name, address, phone number, website URL, and business category. This information is necessary to perform visibility scans and generate reports.

Contact Information: We collect your name, email address, and phone number to communicate with you about your account and deliver reports.

Scan Data: We collect publicly available information about your business from search engines, directories, review sites, and other online platforms. This data is already public — we organize and present it to you in a useful format.

Basic Analytics: We collect basic website usage data (pages visited, time on site) to improve our service. We do not use invasive tracking or third-party advertising trackers.

3. How We Use Your Information

Service Delivery: To scan platforms for your business presence, generate visibility scores, produce audit reports, and deliver monthly monitoring reports.

Communication: To send you your reports, notify you of visibility changes, and respond to your questions.

Service Improvement: To improve our scanning accuracy, scoring algorithms, and platform coverage.

4. What We Do NOT Do

We want to be explicit about what we will never do with your data:

We do not sell your personal or business information to anyone. We do not share your data with advertisers. We do not send unsolicited marketing emails. We do not share your business data with your competitors. We do not use your data to train AI models for third parties.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure. We use encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only. While we implement reasonable security measures, no system is 100% secure. We will notify affected users promptly in the event of a data breach.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies necessary for site functionality. We do not use advertising cookies, social media tracking pixels, or third-party analytics services that track you across the web.

7. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to operate our platform, including cloud hosting, email delivery, and payment processing. These providers process data on our behalf under their respective privacy policies. We do not share more data with them than necessary to provide our service.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You can request a copy of all data we hold about you. You can cancel your account and request deletion of your data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at david@watchlocal.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

WatchLocal.ai is a business service not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by email or through the platform. Your continued use of WatchLocal.ai after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
